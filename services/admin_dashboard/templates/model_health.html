How these metrics are computed

pred_bias_7d = AVG(predicted_points − actual_points) over the last 7 days of graded predictions with edge ≥ 3. Negative = under-prediction.

model_mae_7d = AVG(|predicted_points − actual_points|). vegas_mae_7d = AVG(|line_value − actual_points|) over the same rows.

mae_gap_7d = model_mae_7d − vegas_mae_7d. Positive = model worse than Vegas; this is the primary alert signal (validated against 5 seasons — median 0.39 K in healthy 2024-25 vs 1.44 K in Nov 2025 anomaly).

Verdict thresholds: LOSING_BAD = mae_gap_7d > 2.0 K on ≥3 of last 5 days; LOST_EDGE = mae_gap_7d > 1.0 K on ≥5 of last 7 days; WATCH = mae_gap_7d > 0.5 K (single day); else HEALTHY. INSUFFICIENT_DATA when rolling_n_7d < 20.
